Effortless.

Only Lyric allows you to completely forget about your hearing aid – because once it has been expertly inserted by a hearing care professional who is a fully trained Lyric partner, Lyric can stay in your ear canal for up to four months. There is no need for you to clean and reinsert the device on a daily basis during this time, nor do you have to replace the batteries. You can simply forget you are wearing Lyric at all.

Outstanding sound.
Lyric enables you to enjoy outstanding sound quality because the device is positioned close to the eardrum, making the best possible use of the anatomy of your ear. This provides you with a natural hearing experience –with no feedback or background noise whatsoever.


What makes Lyric so effortless?

  • Lyric stays in your ear canal for up to 4 months
  • No need to replace batteries
  • The device does not need to be cleaned
  • Excellent sound quality thanks to unique technology
  • Natural sound due to proximity to the eardrum
  • Comfortable and secure fit in the ear
