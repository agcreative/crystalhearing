Invisible.

Lyric is inserted deep into the ear canal by a hearing care professional so that it appears completely invisible from the outside. This enable you to enjoy being able to hear again, no matter how close you get to other people and no matter what kind of hearing situation you find yourself in – because Lyric is simply invisible, all the time.

Natural hearing.
The proximity of the device to the eardrum ensures the best possible sound quality. The natural movement of the pinna provides good directional hearing, natural amplification of high frequencies and reduced ambient and wind noise.

Wearing earphones.
Lyric’s positioning in the ear canal also enables you to use earphones, telephones and stethoscopes without any problems – and without any feedback either.
